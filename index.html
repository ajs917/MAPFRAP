AutoDrawer

AutoDrawer is an automated drawer unit designed to be fitted in the house, to support day to day activites. Our product eases accessability to low level storage compartments for those with muscoskeletal injuries. The product is currently in its prototyping phase, keep an eye on this page for all the updates.

The concept behind the product is that at a simple push of a button on the top surface, the device will bring the selected drawer to the top for the user. With a second push of the button, the drawer will be returned to it's original position.

Linear Rail Actuation

The position of the drawer is controlled through the V slot linear rail system depcited above. The system is driven by three stepper motors - two to drive horizontal motion and one for vertical motion.

The stepper motors are driven through an Arduino Uno and A4988 Motor Drivers to accurately control position. Currently the system is being prototyped using GRBL open source software, with positions being demanded by sending G-code.

Bracket & Drawers

A right-angled bracket with a hooked top to mitigate against moments was utilised as the design of choice. The hooked top is made of metal in order to provide the support against bending moments.

The drawers are designed as a compliment of the bracket. The negative of the bracket is incorporated in the drawer design, such that the bracket slides in and up to support the drawer.

Once fully developed, this system will be fully covered to avoid interaction with moving components. Safety is very important to the team, so even with curious grandchildren and pets you can use this device!

Spacious Storage

The unit allows for efficient storage of household items. With our large drawers (that can accomodate up to 5kg load) you will never run out of space for your goods.

The strong aluminium extrusion base means that AutoDrawer can compensate heavy loading, and when fully developed the top can be used as a normal surface.

Power Outage? Electronics malfunction? Fear not - your goods are still accessible! The system has been designed to still operate manually so our users need not worry!
